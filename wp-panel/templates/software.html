General Principles

  • post_max_size must ≥ upload_max_filesize，Nginx client_max_body_size must be consistent
  • max_execution_time 300，temporarily raise to 600 for imports or batch processing. 600
  • MariaDB innodb_buffer_pool_size should be 10%~25%（conservative），MySQL official recommendation up to 50%~70%
  • Redis maxmemory per site approx. 64~128mb，scale proportionally for multi-site but not exceeding 50%
  • Single-site vs. multi-site differs mainly in MariaDB pool and Redis; PHP params depend mainly on site complexity
